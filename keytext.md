## Description
TripSync - The Collaborative Travel Planner

Planning a trip with friends or family shouldn't be stressful. TripSync brings everyone together in one place to plan the perfect trip.

KEY FEATURES

- Collaborative Itineraries: Build day-by-day plans with your travel group. Add activities, set times, and organize by category - from attractions and restaurants to transport and accommodation.

- Interactive Maps: See all your activities pinned on a beautiful map. Color-coded markers make it easy to plan your route and discover nearby attractions.

- Group Polls and Voting: Can't decide where to eat or what to do? Create polls and let the group vote. Supports single choice, multiple choice, and swipe voting.

- Expense Tracking and Splitting: Track shared expenses and see who owes what. Automatic per-person cost splitting takes the math out of group travel.

- Smart Packing Lists: Never forget essentials again. Use pre-built templates for beach, city, or camping trips, or create your own custom lists with progress tracking.

- iCloud Sync: Your trips sync seamlessly across all your Apple devices. Start planning on your iPhone, review on your iPad.

- Privacy First: Your data stays on your device and in your iCloud. We don't collect or sell your information.
