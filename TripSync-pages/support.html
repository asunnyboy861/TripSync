Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Tap the + button on the Trips tab, enter your trip name, destination, and dates, then tap Create.

How do I add activities to my itinerary?

Select a day from the day picker, then tap the + button to add a new activity with a title, category, and optional location.

How do I share a trip with others?

Go to the trip details, tap the share button, choose the access level, and generate a share link to send to your travel companions.

How do I create a poll?

Navigate to the Polls tab within a trip, tap +, enter your question and at least two options, then tap Create.

How does expense splitting work?

Add expenses to your trip, and TripSync automatically calculates the per-person share based on the number of members.

What's included in TripSync Pro?

TripSync Pro includes unlimited trips, iCloud sync, advanced polls, expense splitting, packing templates, and priority support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription in Settings > Your Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device, or through the App Store.
